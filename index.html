Tips for Success

  1. Start early: Be it your Internal Assessments, Extended Essay, or studying for the finals, the early bird gets the worm.
  2. Stay organized: Use digital tools, planners, or apps to keep track of deadlines and requirements.
  3. Practice consistently: Past papers, quizzes, and group studies can be beneficial.
  4. Mental health matters: Take breaks, engage in extracurricular activities, and ensure you have a support system in place.
  5. Engage in CAS creatively: Instead of seeing it as a burden, use CAS as an opportunity to learn new skills and contribute positively to society.
