IB DP Tips

  1. Begin your Extended Essay (EE) early and choose a topic you are passionate about.
  2. For Theory of Knowledge (ToK), focus on real-world applications and examples.
  3. Always backup your Internal Assessment (IA) work and drafts.
  4. Practice time management, especially for exams.
  5. Engage in Group 4 projects actively; it's a great way to learn collaboratively.
